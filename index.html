About Us

Welcome, what started out as a hobby, soon evolved into a business idea. Versus Games SF has been in the planning 2011. Our goal is to fill the need and absence of a dedicated organized play gaming store in San Francisco. We are dedicated to providing bay area and local gamers with frequent gaming tournaments and events involving, card games and tabletop games. We know that there is already a strong gaming community in San Francisco and we very much want to assist the growth and be part of the gaming community.

We offer an eclectic array of games, collectible card games, board games, gaming accessories, concession snacks, specialty gifts and of course Tournament events!

We also hope to provide services to our community and schools in hopes to bridge the understanding and correlation of games with education and social skills.
